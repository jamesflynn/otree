Instructions

You are invited to participate in a research study on decision-making. You will be asked to read a page of instructions. Then you will be asked to make several choices using your computer that will determine the precise amount you will be paid. You will also answer several survey questions.

Participants will receive on average $10. Your payment is based on the actions you select and may be higher or lower than $10. All subjects who complete the study will be paid. Subjects who do not complete the study or demonstrably fail to pay attention to the study may forfeit payment.

We do not guarantee you will receive any benefits from this study beyond the payment you receive for participating.

Your participation in this experiment will take approximately 30 minutes.

If you have read this form and decide to participate in this project, please understand your participation is voluntary and you have the right to discontinue participation at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled. Your individual privacy will be maintained in all published and written data resulting from the study.
